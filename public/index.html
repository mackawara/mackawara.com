Recent Projects

May 2022

Inbert Delivery Service

A fully responsive, full stack web application for a delivery company. Users can book deliveries on the UI. Once the booking is validated (both on the frontend and on the backend) is registered on the database. The bookings manager is notified of the new booking through email and also through a whatsap message. The admin dashaboard fetches list all the bookings from the database and lists them according to order of bookings. This is going to save the client a lot of man hours that they usually spend taling to customers and taking bookings and logging them manually and then notifying the drivers
